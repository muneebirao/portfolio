Get to know me

A blend of artistry, strategy, and a little buttercream.

Faiza

Hello, my name is Faiza Muneeb. I am a Certified Pastry Chef and Social Media Marketing student with a passion for creativity, baking, and digital storytelling.

I specialize in floral cupcake bouquets, custom royal icing cookies, and visually appealing dessert creations that combine artistry with craftsmanship.

In addition to baking, I enjoy creating content that educates, inspires, and connects with audiences. Through my studies and creative projects, I have developed skills in social media marketing, content creation, digital advertising, graphic design, and audience engagement.

My goal is to combine my creative talents with modern marketing strategies to help brands and businesses share their stories effectively.
